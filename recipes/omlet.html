Omlet

Descriptio n

Omlet is a nice and easy food to make. First, you need to put your pan with some hit on it and spread some oil for the omlent not to stick to the pan

Second, get a cup and break the the egg liquid inside and mix it well with any additional cheese or greens and pure it into the pan, now wait a while until the egg start to cook and when the egg is well done
you should flip the egg on the other side for it to cook and after a few seconds there you have it

  • cup
  • oil
  • palte
